My Life is Currency
-------------------

Player is a post-apocalyptic version of the Quaker Oats dude.
	- most prized posession is a cache of Life brand cereal

In the grim future of 2024, neo-capitalists are attempting to regain control of the earth - but they need currency to do it. Currency like... YOUR LIFE.
